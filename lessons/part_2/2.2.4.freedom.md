# 2.2.3 Beyoncé: *Freedom*
*From the genre-defying song cycle, Lemonade*

The song *Freedom* from Beyoncé's 2016 album/film *Lemonade* contains examples of audio sampling that, like the work of Steve Reich in *Different Trains,* invites the voices of the past to lend weight and historical importance to the very personal experience of the artist.
